Solar System SMP

Introducing the ultimate space survival map: Solar System SMP!
You can now have a survival experience on every planet in our solar system.

Compatible with mods, and can be played on java or bedrock.
Includes a hub that allows access to each planet.
Size of each planet ranges from 5000x5000 - 10000x10000.

Planets available to play on are Mercury, Venus, Moon, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
